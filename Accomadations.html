Levex Family Resort

All accommodations face forwards towards the beach and sea and are chic in design. The resort includes premium suites with individual plunge or swimming pools and private sundecks. The resort is home to three restaurants, the Great House for all day dining, the Stone Barn, adult only dining and the Fisherman's Village situated on the waters edge. Guests can also take advantage of two swimming pools, water sports and a nine room spa, The Miraval Life and Balance Spa offering treatments and wellness programs.
